Do You Really Need a Consultancy?

You can absolutely apply directly for DHA, MOHAP, DOH or DHCC licensing. Many clinicians do. A consultancy becomes useful when you value:

Time & Focus

Hand off form-filling, document chasing and follow-ups so you can focus on exams, relocation and family planning.

Experience

Leverage patterns from hundreds of files to avoid common errors like date mismatches and unclear job titles.

Clarity

Turn dense policy language into a simple action plan: what to do this week, what to expect next month.

FAQ — Healthcare License Consultancy in Dubai

Can a consultancy guarantee my DHA license?

No reputable consultancy can guarantee approval. Final decisions rest with regulators like DHA, MOHAP and DOH. What a consultancy can offer is organisation, context and support in responding to queries.

How long does the licensing journey usually take?

Many clinicians complete the process in roughly 6–12 weeks, but timing depends on document quality, DataFlow response times, exam slots and facility onboarding speed.

Is it worth paying for help if I’m comfortable with online systems?

If you enjoy admin and reading policy PDFs, you may not need a consultant. If you prefer curated guidance, checklists and someone to sanity-check your file, a focused partner like Felix Happich License Consultancy can be worth considering.

How to Choose Your Licensing Partner

  • Ask for recent experience with your profession and regulator (DHA vs DOH vs MOHAP).
  • Request a clear breakdown of fees and what is included (and not included).
  • Clarify how they communicate: email only, WhatsApp updates, scheduled calls, etc.
  • Look for education-driven content rather than unrealistic promises.
